Description of Target Audience:
The target audiences are the local business owners and the people who wish to support them and defend them.

Persona #1
Local business owners.
They want to know how they are being helped and if they can recieve any extra of that help and/or help the Chamber of Commerce.

Persona #2
People looking to support their local business owners.
This could be anyone, any age, work experience, or any background. They just have to want to help.

Scenario #1
A business is curious about how the chamber defends them and if they can support the defense.

Scenario #2
Somebody wants to learn how to help their local business owners and the economy.

Scenario #3
Somebody came across a law they thought was unjust and wanted to know what we are doing about it.

Scenario #4
A local business owner wants to know if we can help them get discounts or better deals.

Scenario #5
A bigger company wants to help out some of the local businesses so they look for how they can offer better deals to members of the Chamber of Commerce.
